Reverse Digit Test

When this test starts you will be presented with a sequence of digits appearing at the top of the screen.

Use the numberpad to enter those digits in the reverse of the order they were presented in.

Click the tick symbol, at the bottom right of the numberpad, when done. You will then be presented with the next sequence.
You may also use the backspace symbol, at the bottom left of the numberpad, to delete miss-entered characters.

The first two items are examples, which won't let you enter the wrong answer and will allow you to repeat the sequence.
The numberpad will change from green to black when the examples are finished.

Before starting this test, please make sure that you aren't likely to be disturbed for the next 20 minutes.
This is a short-term memory test and being distracted at the wrong moment could result in you not seeing or forgetting the sequence presented at the start of each test item.

The test gives points for each number you get right and for getting their order correct.
